Romaine Beet Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head of Romaine Lettuce
  • 1/3 cup of coconut chips
  • 1/3 cup of walnuts
  • 1 beet
  • Goat cheese of choice
  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Procedures

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Peel beet and then cube.
  3. Season beet pieces with ½ tablespoon olive oil, ½ teaspoon salt and ¾ teaspoon pepper. Bake for 30 minutes or until fork-tender, then set aside to cool completely.
  4. Toast coconut chips on a pan or in aluminum foil-wrapped tray for approximately 2-3 minutes (or until golden brown). Set aside to cool completely.
  5. Chop walnuts into small chunks.
  6. To make dressing, add balsamic vinegar, sweetener, salt and ground pepper, stir till completely mixed. Then, add the remaining olive oil.
  7. Assemble the salad: Romaine first in the bowl, then the completely cooled coconut chips and beets, with chopped walnuts and grated goat cheese on top, and lastly but not leastly: the glossy dressing. Mix thoroughly and enjoy!
